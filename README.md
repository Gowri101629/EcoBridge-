# EcoBridge-
A revolutionary project in the field of agriculture and farming that follows a smart method to implement sustainable agriculture.
We know that farmers will not divert from their orthodox, inorganic methods of agriculture.
So to bring them on track to sustainable farming is difficult.
We at EcoBridge use a way to make them understand that sustainable farming is not only more eco-friendly but also profitable
Through middlemen for distribution, farmers hardly see profit.
So to fix this middleman issue, while also showing them the benefits in organic farming, we gain trust of the farmers.
As the farmers trust us with higher profits in organic farming, they slowly shift to sustainable practices.
with this transition, we promise a safer farming technique that protects our environment and also benefits our farmers.
When organic products are marketed as "no middleman crops", their demand and value increases, we can spread this much better.
Not only will this benefit the existing farmers, but also interest newer farmers to join the culture.
Through word of mouth and traditional marketing, EcoBridge can make its name big in the field of agriculture.
